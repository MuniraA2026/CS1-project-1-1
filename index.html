Strawberry

Description

A moist and fluffy strawberry cake, bursting with fresh berry flavor and topped with creamy strawberry frosting.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups flour
  • 2 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • ¾ cup butter, softened
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 cup strawberry puree
    • Frosting:
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3 ½ cups powdered sugar
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup milk

Steps

  1. Mix Batter: Cream butter and sugar, add eggs, vanilla, dry ingredients, milk, and strawberry puree.
  2. Bake: Pour into greased 9-inch pans, bake at 350°F for 25-30 minutes. Cool.
  3. Prepare Frosting: Beat butter until creamy, then add powdered sugar, cocoa, vanilla, and milk until smooth and spreadable.

Chocolare Cake

Description

A rich, moist chocolate cake with a decadent, creamy chocolate frosting.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups flour
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup boiling water
    • Frosting:
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3 ½ cups powdered sugar
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • ¼ cup milk

Steps

  1. Batter: Combine dry ingredients, add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Stir in boiling water.
  2. Bake: Pour into greased pans, bake at 350°F for 30-35 minutes.
  3. Frost: Beat butter with powdered sugar, cocoa, vanilla, and milk.

Vanilla Cake

Description

A classic, fluffy vanilla cake with a light, creamy frosting.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups flour
  • 2 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • ¾ cup butter, softened
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla
  • 1 cup milk
    • Frosting:
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2-3 tbsp milk

Steps

  1. Batter: Cream butter and sugar, add eggs, vanilla, flour, baking powder, and milk.
  2. Bake: Pour into greased pans, bake at 350°F for 25-30 minutes.
  3. Frost: Beat butter, powdered sugar, vanilla, and milk until smooth.
